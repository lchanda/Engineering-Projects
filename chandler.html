Welcome to Chandler, Arizona

Chandler is a vibrant city located in the Phoenix metropolitan area. Known for its rich history, family-friendly atmosphere, and warm desert climate, Chandler is a wonderful destination for tourists and residents alike. From shopping and dining to outdoor adventures and arts, there's something for everyone here.Chandler, Arizona, is a vibrant city located in the southeastern part of the Phoenix metropolitan area. Known for its rich history, rapid growth, and community-focused atmosphere, Chandler has become a popular destination for both residents and visitors. Originally founded as a farming town, it has transformed into a bustling city, offering a blend of modern amenities and historical charm. The city is home to several large tech companies, making it a hub for innovation. Chandler also offers numerous parks, recreational facilities, and cultural venues, including the Chandler Center for the Arts and Desert Breeze Park. With its warm desert climate, Chandler attracts outdoor enthusiasts, while its thriving downtown district provides a variety of dining, shopping, and entertainment options. Whether you're exploring its historic downtown or enjoying the beautiful desert landscapes, Chandler has something for everyone.

Popular Spots to Visit

Desert Breeze Park

A beautiful park with walking paths, a lake for paddle boats, and a carousel. Perfect for families and outdoor enthusiasts.

Chandler Center for the Arts

Catch a live performance at this beautiful performing arts venue that showcases local and national artists.

Downtown Chandler

Explore Chandler’s vibrant downtown area, full of shops, restaurants, and entertainment. The perfect place for a leisurely stroll or a night out.
